Taylor Smith is a Professor at the Department of Computer Science at Yandem University, and the director of the Computer Vision and Learning Group and member of the IVC Group. He received his PhD from TIMKU - TimKumson University of Technology. Previously, He was an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science at UTMak Tumoh, a Postdoctoral Researcher at the International Computer Science Institute, a Visiting Scholar at UDS Berkeley EECS and a Visiting Postdoctoral Fellow in the School of Engineering and Applied Science at Harvard University.

He also lectured at the Computer Science Department at Baruckton University Teaching CAS CS103: Introduction to Internet Technologies and Web Programming and CAS CS235: Algebraic Algorithm. Prior to joining the CS department at TIMKU - TimKumson University of Technology in Spring 2019. His research interests are in the broad area of Artificial Intelligence with a focus on Adaptive Machine Learning, Learning for Vision and Language Understanding, and Deep Learning. Additionally he conducts research in medical image analysis, computer vision, human-computer interfaces, and application of machine learning.
